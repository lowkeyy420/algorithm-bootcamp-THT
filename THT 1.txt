Mission to find 'Joe' on my school yearbook and follow his social media 
with Linear search and Binary search approach.

There are 200 students in the school yearbook and each page is sorted out based on 
the student's alphabetical order.

First approach :	//Complexity -> O(n)
1.	Pick up the yearbook
2.	Open the first page of the yearbook
3.	Look at the page 
4.	If Joe is on page
5.		Follow Joe
6.	Else if Joe is not on page (1)
7.		Go to the next page (2)
8.		Go back to line 3
9.	Else
10.		Quit

Second approach :	//Complexity -> O(log n)
1.	Pick up the yearbook
2.	Open to the middle of the yearbook (100)
3.	Look at the page 
4.	If Joe is on page
5.		Follow Joe
6.	Else if Joe is earlier in book 
7.		Open to middle of left half of the book (50)
8.		Go back to line 3
9.	Else if Joe is later in book
10.		Open to middle of right half of the book (150)
11.		Go back to line 3
12.	Else
13.		Quit
